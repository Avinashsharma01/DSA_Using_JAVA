## 💡 Tips for Practice

1. **Time Yourself**: Stick to suggested time limits
2. **No Shortcuts**: Try solving before looking at solutions
3. **Multiple Approaches**: Think of 2-3 ways to solve
4. **Edge Cases**: Always test with corner cases
5. **Optimize**: Start with brute force, then optimize
6. **Explain**: Teach the solution to someone else
7. **Repeat**: Revisit problems you found difficult
